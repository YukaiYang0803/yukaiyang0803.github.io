Yukai Yang

PhD Student, Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Texas A&M University

Hi there! I am Yukai Yang. I am a researcher developing reliable and interpretable machine learning methods for AI for Biology, focusing on the intersection of representation learning and uncertainty quantification.

I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Texas A&M University , advised by Professor Yu Zhang and Professor Yang Shen.

Previously, I worked on Uncertainty Quantification for Large Language Models, advised by Professors Tim Rudner and Marco Morucci. I also conducted research on AI Safety for multi-agent systems with Professor Guang Cheng’s group at UCLA, and worked on scientific machine learning for AI4Bio with Professor Ruqi Zhang. I received my M.S. in Statistics from the University of Chicago , and a Bachelor’s degree (magna cum laude) in Data Science and Mathematics from New York University .
